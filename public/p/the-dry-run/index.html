Publication

The Dry Run

I was challenged to conceptualize a broadsheet publication based on the content of a literary work.

Flowers for Algernon by Daniel Keyes holds a special place in my heart. Taking the themes of the story, I designed a broadsheet publication to test my ability to create a cohesive typographic hierarchy system. At the same time, I chose to diverge from typical publication norms just enough to convey a distance from normalcy while keeping the copy easy to dive into.
